Privacy Policy - Guardian Shield

Last updated: October 2025

All data is processed locally on your device. No data is sent to the internet.

Contact: teknohelping@gmail.com

English

Guardian Shield analyzes the apps installed on your device to assess their security levels. All data, including app names, permissions, and installation sources, is processed locally on your device and is never sent to the internet.

Certain features of Guardian Shield require in-app purchases processed via Google Play. No payment or personal information is collected by the app itself.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding your privacy, please contact us at the email above.
